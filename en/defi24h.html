24hr challenge

The 24hr challenge is a artistic creation test on a 24h period. A theme is given and it is expected to make a piece in relation with the theme. In small teams, we are in competition during a whole day and night. Through out the entirety of the challenge are prepared missions and constraints. Each activity earns points. The team with most points and the best piece wins the competition. This year, a movie that won the first position.

My work

With my team, we have chosen to realise a audio story. We wanted it to be a tribute to our college and it's teachers. For that, we made each character as a reference to a professor. I helped to write the scenario and the characters. All of that to make the world complete and credible. After wich, I lended my voice to a character (the king by the way). Once all the audio was recorded, I edited a segment of the story on PremièrePro and Audition. We then joined every one's work, wich gives this funny story.
