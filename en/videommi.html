College presentation video

During my formation, I had the opportunity to realise a video that was used to present our college. It was presented during open day at the occasion of a WebTv for wich I also participated. The video introduces three of my teachers, Pascal Chatonnay, Xavier Senante and Jean-marie Fino. They present the upsides od the formation and alsoMMI strengths by the end of the two years. The video also talks about the semestre in Canada and about the lessons distribution.

Adapt the content

The video was reworked to correspond to different plateforms. Particularly for the Facebook page, for wich the video has become the banner. There where the sound is less important, I removed the interviews to show only the cut scenes. They present the departement and what we do there. The video can now be played in a loop. It is still editied to be in harmonie with the music. In case the user wants to activate the sound.
