What a World

What a world is born from the want to help people discover the world and facilitate the exchange between different populations. It's goal was to help prepare journeys and holidays easly. The website offered a great number of information on each country, and past travelers could give their thoughts and advices on the destination. Among the many projet propositions, ours was the most popular within our college.

My work

In groups of three and in four months, we did the conception, the design and the website's developement. I took care of the frontend development and the marketing. For the occasion we have opened Facebook pages and Instagram accounts. On wich we published regularly and have boosted a publication.
