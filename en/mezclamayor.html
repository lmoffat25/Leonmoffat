Mezcla Mayor

Mezcla mayor is a presentation website for a latin music group. The website's goal is to give visibility to the group and help professionals to contact them. For this projet, I took care of the textual content of the communication and the coding in html / css / js. I was also nammed head of team to assure the project's success.

My work

My principal role was to manage the project's communication. I studied their communication actions, their presence online and proposed many options to improve the group's visibility. I wrote many folders to help our client to manage his website. But I also revisited their Facebook page managment and created a Instagram account. It's on these places that they publish their latest news.
